Our Mission

ContinuM is a technology startup based in San Diego, CA. It is a comprehensive collaboration solution that provides all types of organizations and individuals with the ability to centralize the way they work. ContinuM's purpose is to augment everyone's success by streamlining efficiency and productivity. ContinuM automatically captures and preserves relationships established through the course of work which then facilitates future opportunities and success.

Meet the Team

Joy Hou

Co-Founder, CEO

Joy immigrated from Taiwan at age 10 and began learning real estate at age 11. Before graduating from high school, she acquired and asset managed a restaurant, motel, hotel, and residential land. Joy spent 10 years on Wall St (DLJ, Lehman, Barclays) where she closed $10 billion of RE transactions. Joy graduated from Cornell Hotel School in 1998 with distinction. She speaks 'International' food and enjoys quality time with family.

Edwin Liu

Co-Founder, CTO

Edwin is trapped in a vicious cycle of solutions that inspire new problems, from software for indexing reading highlights to home appliance microcontrollers to prototyping\/fabricating car parts with 3d printers. Edwin graduated from UMD with a Computer Science degree before gaining commercial real estate experience from Peracon and Renaissance bank asset platform. Other experience includes Weatherbug and UMD's CATT Lab. Edwin enjoys building, racing, and fixing cars.

Sandee Phillips

Vice President

Sandee is responsible for the firm's human resources and data management. Sandee has over 30 years experience in banking operations garnered from working in the financial industry, including working most recently for JP Morgan Chase. Sandee is certified in human resource management from the University of Redlands. Aside from her passion for human resources and data management, Sandee enjoys traveling near and far, along with taking walks on the beach with her beloved dog and saving her memories from her travels by scrapbooking.
